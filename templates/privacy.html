Privacy Policy

At Spendly, your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe when you use our expense tracking application.

1. Introduction

We collect only the information needed to provide and improve the Spendly service. By using the application, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect account information you provide during registration, such as your name and email address. We also store the expense data you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. In addition, we gather basic usage analytics to understand how the service is used.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide the core expense tracking service, to improve and personalize your experience, and to send critical updates about your account or important changes to the service.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is transmitted over encrypted connections and stored securely. We retain your data only for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

5. Cookies

We use cookies for essential purposes such as session management and remembering your preferences. These cookies are necessary for the application to function correctly.

6. Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with essential third parties, such as hosting providers, and only under data processing agreements that require them to protect your information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may also delete your account entirely, which removes your associated information from our systems.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you so you can review the updated terms before continuing to use the service.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@spendly.app.
